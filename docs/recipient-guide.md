# Recipient Guide ✍️

This section is for people who receive a signing invitation.

## Step 1 - Open invitation link safely
- **Action**: Open invitation from your email and verify sender/domain.
- **Expected result**: External portal opens.
- **If not**: Ask initiator to resend invitation.
- **Screenshot**: No screenshot needed, because mailbox UI is outside SignBox.

## Step 2 - Select country and signing method
- **Action**: Choose your country and one available method.
- **Expected result**: Authentication starts.
- **If not**: Ask initiator/admin to verify enabled methods for your country.
- **Screenshot**:

<p align="center">
  <img src="../assets/annotated/step-13-recipient-country-method.png" width="900" alt="Recipient country and method selection">
  <br><em>Figure 1 — Choose country and signing method in external portal.</em>
</p>

## Step 3 - Confirm signing in provider app
- **Action**: Approve request in Smart-ID/eID/Mobile-ID app/device.
- **Expected result**: Portal status updates after successful confirmation.
- **If not**: Retry once and check mobile/device network.
- **Screenshot**: No screenshot needed, because provider confirmation view is outside SignBox UI.

## Step 4 - Download signed file
- **Action**: Use download option after completion.
- **Expected result**: Signed document is downloaded.
- **If not**: Ask initiator to check `History` status.
- **Screenshot**: No screenshot needed, because final download screen is configuration-dependent and not stable across tenants.

> [!WARNING]
> Do not share recipient invitation links.
